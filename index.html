About Belt General Business P.L.C

Belt General Business P.L.C is an import, trading, and transport business enterprise established in 2001. We believe in the development and optimal utilization of water resources as key factors for poverty reduction, social growth, and national economic progress.

Our company supplies top-quality equipment in the fields of:

  • Energy
  • Water
  • Irrigation
  • Agriculture
  • Engineering Laboratory

We collaborate with international manufacturers known for quality and efficiency, operating with state-of-the-art technology and sophisticated testing equipment. Belt General Business P.L.C is one of the fastest-growing and most technically competent suppliers in the industry, with a strong business network across Africa, Asia, Europe, and America.

Belt Line of Business

Water Supply

I- Electro-Mechanical Services

  • Design, installation, testing & commissioning of water supply and sanitation systems.
  • Design, installation & commissioning of pumping systems for various applications.
  • Installation & commissioning of urban & industrial water treatment plants (electro-mechanical, biological & chemical treatment processes).
  • Borehole construction, testing, and development.
  • Rehabilitation and development of existing water supply systems.

II- Product Sales (Import, wholesale distribution & supply)

  • Various types of pumps for domestic, public, and industrial applications.
  • Urban water transmission lines, storage reservoirs & distribution systems.
  • Pipes (Steel, PVC, PPR & DCI), casing tubes (PVC, Steel), valves, fittings, and water meters.
  • Generators and other electro-mechanical water supply equipment.
